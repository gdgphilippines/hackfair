About HackFair

HackFair 2015 is an interactive two-day event where thirty individuals and teams are given the opportunity to showcase their App/Website/IoT. HackFair first started in 2012 in Korea and is now present in seven countries namely - Korea, Japan, Vietnam, Indonesia, Pakistan, Thailand, and the Philippines. Ideas can range from health, education, productivity, music, arts and culture, to social impact. Selected projects are invited for the exhibit and the mini-conference.

About GDG HackFair Philippines 2015

GDG Philippines is organizing the first GDG HackFair in the Philippines this December 19-20, 2015 at Mind Museum, Bonifacio Global City.
Submit your mobile, web, or IOT project on or before December 12.
