About HackFair

GDG Philippines is organizing the first GDG HackFair in the Philippines this December 19-20, 2015 at the Mind Museum, Bonifacio Global City.

HackFair 2015 is an interactive two-day event where thirty App/Website/IoT that were created using Google Technologies are given the opportunity to be featured.

Apply to be one of the thirty teams/individuals to feature your app/website/IoT! Ideas can range from health, education, productivity, music, arts and culture, social impact, etc. Selected projects are invited for the exhibit and the mini-conference.

FAQs

General Questions

When and where is HackFair going to be?
HackFair is going to be on December 19-20 at the Mind Museum, Bonifacio Global City.
Is HackFair a competition?
No, HackFair is not a competition. It is a two-day event where thirty selected apps/website/IoT that were created using Google Technologies are given the opportunity to be featured.
How do I feature my app/website/IoT at the HackFair?
Apply at http://hackfair.gdgph.org/

Deadline is on or before 11:59pm December 12, 2015.
Can I join HackFair without applying as one of the exhibitors?
Yes! Register here: http://hackfair.gdgph.org/
What are the perks of being selected as one of the thirty to showcase idea at Google HackFair?
As one of the selected thirty, you get to have a special space to exhibit your idea on both December 19 and 20. Not only that, you will also get to interact with people from the GDG community and the tech community. Apply now! http://hackfair.gdgph.org/

Application Related Questions

What Google technologies must be used in order to qualify for application?
Listed below but not limited to:
  • Android
  • Android Wear
  • AngularJS
  • Brillo/Weave
  • Cardboard
  • Chrome/HTML5/Polymer
  • Dart Lang
  • Eddystone
  • Firebase
  • Google Analytics
  • Google App Engine
  • Google Compute Engine
  • Google Maps
At least one Google Technology per application
Can I join as an individual?
Yes, you can apply as an individual.
Is there an age limit?
There is no age limit.
What are the categories for the app entries?
Listed below but not limited to:
  • Health
  • Productivity
  • Social Impact
  • Music
  • Arts and Culture
Is there a limit to the number of app entries?
No, there are no limits to the number of entries. You can submit more than one entry.
What is the criteria for selection?
A screening committee will be reviewing the projects. Criteria includes usefulness of the app and Google technologies used.
Once selected, do I need to attend both days (December 19 and 20)?
No but it is better if there will be at least one person per day from your team so you can showcase your project to most attendees.
Where can I learn how to develop my ideas?
To help you in your projects, GDG Philippines will be hosting HackTime events.

There will be talks about different Google technologies and time for hands-on development.

In Manila, our HackTime will be on December 5 at Globe Corporate Showroom, Valero Street, Makati City.

Register for HackTime by clicking "HackTime registration": http://hackfair.gdgph.org/
